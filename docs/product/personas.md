# Personas

- AI governance lead: monitors AI inventory, policy posture, and emerging risks.
- Internal auditor: builds evidence-backed reports and traces results to logged events.
- Compliance specialist: reviews regulatory policy packs, including EU AI Act mappings.
- Model risk manager: investigates assessments, grounding, incidents, and model drift.
- Integration owner: monitors how applications invoke models and controls failures.
- Data administrator: connects DQI event sources, controls access, and audits usage.
