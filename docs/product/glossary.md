# Glossary

- **Semantic request:** Backend-neutral selection of governed metric, dimension, time, and filter IDs.
- **Query IR:** Validated intermediate representation compiled deterministically into backend DSL.
- **Metric:** Versioned business calculation with unit, grain, aggregation, tests, and ownership.
- **Dimension:** Governed field used to filter, group, or segment a metric.
- **Provenance:** Evidence connecting a displayed value to semantic version, query, source, and execution.
- **Widget:** Versioned reusable analytical block containing query intent and visual encoding.
- **Policy:** Reviewable detection specification, potentially proposed from natural language.
- **Grounding:** Constraint that claims and numbers map to available authorised evidence.
